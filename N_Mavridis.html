Nikolaos Mavridis

About:
Nikolaos Mavridis is the founder and director of the Interactive Robots and Media Lab, and a PhD graduate from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology He is also an assistant professor of research at the Computer Science department, New York University Poly, and adjunct researcher at NCSR Demokritos.
